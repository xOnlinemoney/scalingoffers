This Independent Contractor Agreement (the "Agreement") is entered into by and between True Capital Holdings, LLC d/b/a Scaling Offers ("Company") and the undersigned contractor ("Webinar Host").

1. Definitions

"Offer Owner" refers to the client of the Company whose product or service is being sold via webinar.

"Webinar" refers to a live sales presentation, typically 1-2 hours in duration, conducted immediately following the Company's proprietary Sales Process.

"Repitch" refers to a follow-up session, typically 1 hour in duration, conducted the day after the Webinar to answer FAQ and assist prospective buyers with the funding process.

"Top-Line Revenue" means gross revenue actually collected and received by the Offer Owner's payment processor. Revenue is counted only when payment is confirmed.

"Confidential Information" includes but is not limited to sales processes, scripts, pricing models, client lists, business strategies, proprietary methods, trade secrets, and technical data.

"Sales Process" refers to the Company's proprietary methodology for conducting webinars and closing sales.

2. Scope of Services

The Webinar Host agrees to perform the following services as an independent contractor:

  • Co-host live Webinars (1-2 hours) with Offer Owners utilizing the Company's Sales Process.
  • Conduct Repitch sessions (approx. 1 hour) the following day to address FAQs and guide clients through funding.
  • Adhere strictly to Company compliance standards and scripts.
  • Act solely as an independent contractor, not an employee.

3. Compensation

In consideration for the Services, Company shall pay Webinar Host as follows:

  • Webinar Only: 2% of Top-Line Revenue generated from the Webinar.
  • Webinar + Repitch: 3% of Top-Line Revenue generated if both sessions are completed.

Revenue is recognized only when payment is actually received from the client. Payments are remitted semi-monthly on the 3rd and 17th of each month. No other benefits, insurance, or compensation shall be provided.

4. Confidentiality / NDA

Webinar Host agrees to maintain strict confidentiality of all Confidential Information. Information shall be used solely for the purpose of performing services under this Agreement. This obligation remains in effect for five (5) years following termination, and indefinitely for trade secrets. Upon termination, all materials must be returned or destroyed.

5. Non-Solicitation & Non-Circumvention

During the term of this Agreement and for a period of two (2) years following termination:

  • Webinar Host shall not work individually, independently, or directly with any Offer Owner introduced by the Company. Violation of this clause shall result in liquidated damages of $250,000 per occurrence.
  • Webinar Host shall not solicit any employees, contractors, or clients of the Company.

6. Non-Disparagement

Webinar Host agrees not to make, publish, or communicate any disparaging or negative statements regarding the Company, its owners, staff, clients, Offer Owners, or affiliates in any forum (public, private, or social media). Violation of this clause shall result in liquidated damages of $100,000 per occurrence.

7. Intellectual Property

All work product, recordings, scripts, and materials created or used in connection with the Services shall be the sole and exclusive property of the Company. Webinar Host hereby assigns all rights, title, and interest to the Company.

8. Term & Termination

This Agreement may be terminated by either party with thirty (30) days written notice. The Company may terminate immediately for material breach. Sections 4, 5, 6, 7, 9, 10, and 11 shall survive termination.

9. Dispute Resolution

Any dispute arising out of this Agreement shall be settled by binding arbitration administered by the American Arbitration Association (AAA) in accordance with its Commercial Arbitration Rules. Judgment on the award rendered by the arbitrator(s) may be entered in any court having jurisdiction thereof. The seat of arbitration shall be Miami-Dade County, Florida.

10. Attorney's Fees

In the event of any legal action or arbitration to enforce or interpret this Agreement, the non-prevailing party shall pay the prevailing party's reasonable attorney's fees, costs, and expenses.

11. Indemnification

Webinar Host agrees to indemnify, defend, and hold harmless the Company against any claims, losses, or damages arising from Webinar Host's breach of this Agreement, negligence, or misconduct.

12. Governing Law

This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the State of Florida. Venue for any disputes shall lie exclusively in Miami-Dade County, Florida.

13. General Provisions

This Agreement constitutes the entire understanding between the parties. Amendments must be in writing. If any provision is found invalid, the remainder shall continue in effect. This Agreement may not be assigned by Webinar Host without written consent. Electronic signatures shall be considered valid and binding. Force majeure applies to events beyond reasonable control.
