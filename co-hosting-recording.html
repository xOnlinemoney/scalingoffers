Webinar Flow

You Don't Need to Sell

Your role is to bring expertise, clarity, and proof. We lead the room, manage the flow, and handle enrollment. Here's exactly how the webinar flows so you know what to expect.

01
Host

You Warm Up the Room

You keep camera off. You welcome attendees and set the stage. Client joins 5 minutes after you start.

02
Host

Introduce Client

You frame the Client's authority and experience so when they enter the room the credibility is already built.

03
Client

Client Shares Their Story

Client walks through where they started, the problem they faced, what wasn't working, and the transformation they achieved.

04
Client

Client Explains Their Framework

They explain why their system works — what makes it sustainable, repeatable, and different from surface-level tactics.

05
Client

Client Shows Proof

Client shares their screen and walks through their real client results. Before → implementation → outcome. Screenshots, dashboards, wins, testimonials.

06
Host

You Re-Engage the Room

You reignite the chat, reinforce why the client's story matters, and frame the core problem for attendees.

07
Client

Client Teaches One Key Insight

Client delivers a short educational piece — one quick-win concept that makes the problem feel solvable. High-level, not a full workshop.

08
Host

You Handle the Pitch

You walk through the offer, deliverables, investment, financing, and next steps. The client doesn't pitch.

09
Both

Live Q&A

Client answers niche and implementation questions. You handle objections, timing, financing, and enrollment questions.

10
Host

You Close the Room

You post the final CTA, prompt financing applications, and invite the client's closing remarks.

Quick Reference

Your Role at a Glance

Review this checklist before every session. Consistency in the pre-webinar setup leads to conversion in the close.

Before (30 Min Prior)

  • Test mic, camera & internet speed connection
  • Open Q&A panel & webinar platform admin
  • Review offer details & ready CTA links

Live

  • High energy welcome message in Chat
  • Edify & introduce the Owner enthusiastically
  • Reinforce urgency during the Close pitch

After

  • Post CTA link one final time before end
  • Thank attendees and close room
  • Quick 5-minute debrief call with Owner
