7-Day Posting & Story Blueprint

Build hype, remove doubts, correct misconceptions, answer fears, show proof, and create urgency for the LIVE class.

Use your own voice and personality. Just follow each day's theme + structure.

Day 1 — Announcement

Goal: Let people know something big is coming.

Story 1 (Talking Head)

"A lot of you keep asking how I built my life through [niche]... so I'm going LIVE next week breaking down everything I did — step-by-step — even if you don't have a big following, certifications, or XYZ."

"This is probably the only time I'll teach this. Link below."

Story 2

Theme: "Most people say they want more, but never show up for opportunities that matter." (Your own wording)

Story 3 (Lifestyle/Clip)

Text overlay: "This came from learning one skill once."

Link Attached

Day 2 — Limiting Beliefs

Goal: Remove the excuses people hide behind.

Key Beliefs to Address:

  • "I need a big following."
  • "This may not work for me."
  • "I don't have experience."
  • "I need certifications."
Story 1

Pick belief and explain why it isn't required.

Story 2

Your own story or someone you know proving that belief wrong.

Story 3 (CTA)

"Register here — I'll break this down live."

Link Attached

Day 3 — Misconceptions

Goal: Correct the myths holding people back.

Misconceptions to hit:

  • • "The space is oversaturated."
  • • "I won't make any money."
  • • "You need luck."
Story 1

Pick ONE misconception and break it down.

Story 2

"People get paid for solving problems, not XYZ."

Story 3

Theme: "If you keep believing the wrong things, you'll keep doing the wrong things."

Story 4

CTA + Link attached.

Day 4 — Q&A / Fear Handling

Goal: Address the real hesitation — fear.

Story 1 (Question Box)

"What's actually stopping you from coaching online/offering your service?"

Stories 2–4 (Answers)

Answer 2–4 questions. Focus on fear-based ones:

Fear of failing
Fear of being judged
Fear of not knowing enough
Fear of charging money
Story 5 (CTA)

"We're covering everything live — link here."

Day 5 — Pain vs Desire

Goal: Create the emotional shift.

Only 2–3 stories (Retention)
Story 1: The Pain

"What happens if nothing changes?"

  • • Same job
  • • Same income
  • • Same routine
  • • Same frustration

(Keep it short)

Story 2: The Desire

"What life looks like when you DO take action."

Use lifestyle clip, transformation, or simple talking head.

"This live class shows the exact steps to go from Story 1 → Story 2."

Link Attached

Day 6 — Social Proof

Goal: Show that normal people win.

Story 1

Client win, transformation (before & after), or progress screenshot.

Story 2

Theme: "They started where you're at right now."

"We're almost at the live class — grab your spot."

Link Attached

Goal: Drive maximum attendance. Don't let them forget.

MORNING

"Live class is TODAY — last chance to register."

Countdown Sticker Link
AFTERNOON

Answer FAQs:

• "Do I need a following?" No
• "Do I need experience?" No
• "Is it beginner friendly?" Yes
• "Is this the only one?" Yes
Link Attached
1 HOUR

"We're going live in 1 hour — final call to register."

Link
15 MINS

Behind-the-scenes clip (Zoom or slides).

"Going live in 15 minutes — join here."

Guidelines For All 7 Days

  • Post 2–5 story frames daily
  • At least 1 talking-head story each day
  • Always attach the link
  • Mix lifestyle, talking-head, proof, and text stories
  • DM anyone who interacts with link (polls, replies, Q&A)
  • Highlight consistently:

    • – It's live & limited

    – It's for [niche audience] wanting to monetize

    – No XYZ needed

    – This is the opportunity they've been waiting for
